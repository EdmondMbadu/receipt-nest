FAQ

Questions, meet answers

Is my data private?

Yes. Receipts are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data, and you can export or delete everything at any time.

How do receipts get into ReceiptNest AI?

You can forward emails, upload PDF or photo receipts, or connect supported integrations. Everything lands in one organized inbox.

Can I export for taxes or accounting?

Absolutely. Export any time by month, category, or tag to CSV or PDF and share with your accountant.

What happens if I cancel?

You keep access to your data. Pro features are disabled, but you can still view and export your existing receipts.

Is this accounting software?

No. ReceiptNest AI is a spending clarity tool, not a bookkeeping platform. If you need invoicing, payroll, or double-entry accounting, tools like Wave or QuickBooks are better suited. ReceiptNest AI is for people who just want to know where their money went - and be organized when tax time comes.

How is this different from Wave or Expensify?

Wave and Expensify are accounting and expense management platforms built for businesses. ReceiptNest AI is built for individuals who work for themselves and want spending clarity without the complexity. We're also privacy-first - we never sell or share your financial data.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can start on Free and upgrade to Pro when you need unlimited receipts and advanced tools.

Do you sell my data?

Never. Your receipts are encrypted, never shared with third parties, and never used to train AI models. You can export or permanently delete all your data at any time.
